Anslate

Anslate is an app that help patient and doctor communicate in different languages (English and Bahasa Indonesia) by live translation, word correction, medical term explanation, transcription, and conversation history.

Download now


Speech-to-text transcription, live translation,
and Word Suggestion based on text transcription.


Different language and accent make user face difficulty
to explain their health condition clearly to doctor.
We provide word suggestions if there is any mistake from transcription results
and also help live conversation translation for both of patient and doctor’s languages.



Speak or choose the answer based
on doctor question


The patient has difficulty answering closed questions which
leads ambiguous to answer
We help to classify doctor question based on type of answer,
a simple answer or range-like answer so the answer would be more precise



Conversation history with
Medical term information & explanation


Patient tend to forget doctor’s explanation and couldn’t understand some medical statement easily, especially because of language barriers.
If the user want to consult with doctor, using history user can check
again what the conversation before with the doctor so user will not be missed
with the detail. We also provide medical term information to get definitions
and detailed information about medical terminology in the conversation
so the user can understand.
